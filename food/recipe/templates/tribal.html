Tribal Recipes

Mahua Laddu

  • 1 cup mahua flowers
  • 1 cup jaggery
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Steps:

  1. Clean the mahua flowers and remove the stems.
  2. Grind the jaggery and mahua flowers together to make a smooth paste.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan and add the mahua-jaggery paste. Cook on low heat until the mixture thickens.
  4. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  5. When the mixture is cool enough to handle, shape into small balls.
  6. Let the laddus cool completely before storing in an airtight container.

Paat Badi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps:

  1. Soak urad dal in water for 4-5 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind the dal into a smooth paste.
  3. Add salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and asafoetida to the paste and mix well.
  4. Take a clean, dry cloth and spread it out.
  5. Wet your hands and take a small portion of the dal paste.
  6. Shape the paste into a small, round disc and place it on the cloth.
  7. Repeat the process until all the paste is used up. Make sure the discs do not touch each other.
  8. Let the discs dry in the sun for 1-2 days until they become hard and crispy.
  9. Once dry, store the paat badi in an airtight container.
  10. To cook, deep fry the paat badi in hot oil until they puff up and become golden brown.
  11. Serve with your favorite curry or chutney.

Bongulo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup jaggery powder
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Ghee for greasing

Steps:

  1. Dry roast sesame seeds, peanuts, cashew nuts, and raisins separately and keep them aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix rice flour, jaggery powder, grated coconut, and cardamom powder.
  3. Add the dry fruits and nuts to the bowl and mix well.
  4. Gradually add water to the mixture and knead it into a soft dough.
  5. Grease a steaming plate with ghee and spread the dough evenly on it.
  6. Steam the dough for 15-20 minutes on medium flame.
  7. Once done, let it cool and cut into small pieces.
  8. Serve with tea or coffee.
